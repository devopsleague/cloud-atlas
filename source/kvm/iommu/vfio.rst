- `VT-d, vfio and GPU passthrough, Virtualization in a nutshell (RHEL8) <https://blog.sakuragawa.moe/vt-d-vfio-and-gpu-passthrough-virtualization-in-a-nutshell-rhel8/>`_ 这篇是最新在RHEL8上部署GPU passthrough
