OpenShift部署GitLab架构
================================

GitLab Operator是一个遵循 `Kubernetes Operator pattern <https://kubernetes.io/docs/concepts/extend-kubernetes/operator/>`_ 的安装和管理方式，这是专用于 OpenShift 部署GitLab的方式。另外一种在纯 :ref:`k8s_deploy_gitlab` 则采用 :ref:`helm`
